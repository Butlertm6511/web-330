I love running


I enjoy running, and have loved since I was in elementary. I was a walk-on when I attended Adams State College, now University, and came in only knowing the 100 and 200, but by the time I finished, I had two NCAA II National titles and 19-All Americans. My main event ended up being the heptathlon. Which required me to learn how to hurdle, long jump, high jump, and learn to throw the shot put and javelin. I also did the relays. Athletics has shown that when you don't have all the pieces, you step up for the team and learn how to make the pieces you do have work and give the best of yourself for the team. That can come in the form of taking the time to learn a new skill, passing on the skills you know to strengthen the team, and being humble enough to step down when you know someone is better suited for the task.
